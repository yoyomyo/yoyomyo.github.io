Step 3 Use NAND gates to detect numbers

NAND gate: if everything is one, NAND output is 0. If there is a single 0, output is 1.

Each NAND needs to detect one of the following number:

600 (left most NAND)10 0101 1000
60110 0101 1001
60510 0101 1101
628 (right most)10 0111 0100

Can disregard the most significant bit because it's always 1

Step 3 Testing horizontal sync

(see previous steps on testing the counters for wiring)

Once the counter reaches 10 0000 0000, slow down the frequency to 1 or 2Hz, and wait for the diodes to go out one by one. Note that when the counter reaches 10 0101 1101 (605), all three diodes will go out.

If your last diode stays lit at 628, you may need to unplug a wire
